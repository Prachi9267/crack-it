Hackathon Details

1.) Google Girl Hackathon

Registration: Around Jan-March

About: Girl Hackathon is a program for women students in computer science and allied courses across India[cite: 163]. [cite_start]It has been designed to provide a platform for women in the engineering campus space to showcase their coding skills and promote creative solutions for real-time technical challenges.

Procedure

Round 1: Google Online Challenge
  • The first round is a coding round where 2 questions will be given based on DSA.
  • Topics covered include stacks, binary search, and bit manipulation.The difficulty level can be LeetCode medium-hard with a time limit of 1 hour to solve both questions.
  • Participants were judged on their performance in the coding challenge alongside their individual profile screening through resumes.
Round 2: Google Learning Cohorts Workshops
  • Selected participants from Round 1 were divided into different learning cohorts.
  • These workshops are followed by a DSA coding round where participants solve 2 questions based on varied topics such as divide & conquer.The time limit is 1 hour and the difficulty is LeetCode medium-hard.
Round 3: Ideathon Round
  • After the second round, the hackathon enters its pre-final phase.
  • The primary problem statement is subdivided into 3 themes, and participants are required to choose one and develop a solution for it.
The Grand Finale: Presentation round
  • For the final presentation, participants create a presentation on Google Slides showcasing their idea along with a demo of the working prototype.

Tips: Keep practicing DSA and give contests to get a stronghold over it.Do not limit yourself to a particular stack; develop an attitude to solve problems through whatever means necessary.
