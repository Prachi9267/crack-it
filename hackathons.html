Google Girl Hackathon

About

Google Girl Hackathon is a program for women students in computer science and allied courses across India. It provides a platform to showcase coding skills and propose creative solutions to real-world challenges.

Timeline:

Registration around Jan-Feb

Procedure

  1. Round 1: Online Coding Challenge
    2 questions (DSA: stacks, binary search, bit manipulation). Difficulty: LeetCode medium-hard. Duration: 1 hour.
  2. Round 2: Learning Cohorts + Coding Challenge
    Workshops followed by 2 DSA questions. Difficulty: medium-hard.
  3. Round 3: Ideathon
    Teams choose a theme and propose solutions.
  4. Grand Finale: Presentation
    Present solution + prototype demo.

Tips: Practice DSA consistently, attempt contests, and keep an open mind about tech stacks.
